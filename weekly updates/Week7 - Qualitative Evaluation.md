# Think Aloud 

> > As the game is still in active development, we primarily used the think-aloud method during playtesting sessions. This allowed us to collect direct, qualitative feedback from participants in real time, capturing their thoughts, reactions, and points of confusion as they interacted with the prototype, which will inform future iterations of the design.

# Summary of areas requiring adjustments

**Bugs**

- Enemy goes to light switch even if player toggles it
- NPC 1 froze after player left room 1
- User can close door when standing in it

**Design / UX**

- Slowly introduce mechanics (patrol/sprint w/1 enemy in room → opening door w/1 enemy → light switch → key to unlock → portal)
- Tutorial needed to explain gameplay (lots of mechanics/item interactions)
- For locked doors, make it so unlocking door also opens it, rather than unlock → open
- Auto pickup keys when stepping over them

**Controls**

- Move restart key further away from WASD

**UI**

- Add stamina bar

**Visual / Feel**

- Increase door size
